• Headers: <h1> to <h6>
  • Text or blocks:
    • <p> for text paragraphs
    • <span> for inline blocks
    • <div> for blocks or containers
  • Text formatting: <em> for italics, and <strong> for bold
  • Lists: Ordered with <ol>, unordered with <ul>, items with <li>
  • Media: <img> (self-closing) for images, <video> for videos
